Tips to deal with Panic Attacks


1. Breathe in as slowly, deeply and gently as you can, through your nose.


2. Breathe out slowly, deeply and gently through your mouth


3. Count steadily from one to five on each in-breath and each out-breath.


4. Find 5 things you can see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you can hear, 2 things you can smell, and one thing you can taste.
